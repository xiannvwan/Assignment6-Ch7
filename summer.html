Summer Flower - Yellow Lady's Slippers

Yellow Lady’s Slipper facts

  • 4–24 inches tall
  • Perennial
  • Native plant
  • Grows in wet, shaded deciduous woods, swamps, and bogs
  • An orchid
  • Official flower of Door County
